I CAN'T BELIEVE YOU

In I Can't Believe You,, the viewer is invited to a polite conversation with the artist. The viewer's laptop displays a live video feed of me, sitting across from the camera, and the viewer and I are connected via a two-way audio signal. The viewer's phone is a virtual "glass of water."

By throwing the water at their laptop screen, I'm doused in their glass of water and the connection terminates. I'm then immediately connected to the next viewer in the queue. This is an online performance piece that ran briefly in May 2021. Future performances are being arranged.
